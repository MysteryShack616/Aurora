Feedback Based On Survey Done Through Google Forms




  • Based on the survey, majority of people never created their own websites before.
  • The cause of this because they don’t have basics about programming at all.
  • Designing their own website is a hassle and requires a lot of skills which lots of people don't want to.





  • Majority of peoples have used WordPress.com comparing to other websites.
  • WordPress are easier to use and provides many pre-design templates compared to other websites.





  • For WordPress and Wix.com, pre-design templates made available.
  • However for html & CSS , users have to design their own websites to allow them use their creativity and freedom to design their own websites.





  • Most of the people agreed that it’s not too hard or too easy to edit their website’s content after finishing their page.
  • It is because you have to find your content in the page (using html & css) which is not so easy and a bit hard to find.
  • However, for pre-design templates (Refferring to WordPress & Wix), it is super easy to edit your content of your page





  • Surveys have also shown that a lot of people are unsure about the quality of the result because they maybe already achieve their goal but still have things to add.
  • But there is also a group of people that is very satisfied
  • There are also a very little minority of people that are dissatisfied with the result because their main goal cannot be achieved.


*survey was done by non-developers who have tried using atleast 1 tools of the website creator*
